5-Step Intelligence Protocol

Each flagged instance passes through a rigorous multi-stage pipeline before classification and response recommendations are issued.

01
Source
Aggregate data from social media, news outlets, forums, and public communication channels across the Dutch digital landscape.
Data Ingestion
02
Verify
Cross-reference flagged content against linguistic models, contextual databases, and historical pattern analysis for validation.
NLP Analysis
03
Protocol
Apply the IHRA working definition framework and Dutch legal standards to determine classification boundaries.
Framework Match
04
Classify
Assign a severity tier (1–5) based on threat level, reach, intent analysis, and potential for real-world harm escalation.
Tier Assignment
05
Act
Generate response recommendations, alert stakeholders, file reports with relevant authorities, and update the intelligence database.

Severity Tier System

Incidents are assigned one of five severity tiers, each carrying distinct response protocols and escalation procedures.

1
Microaggression Low
Subtle stereotypes, casual prejudice, or unconscious bias in language. Logged for pattern analysis.
20%
2
Coded Rhetoric Moderate
Dog-whistle language, conspiracy references, or plausibly deniable antisemitic tropes and narratives.
40%
3
Overt Hate Speech Elevated
Explicit antisemitic statements, slurs, dehumanizing language, or direct group-targeted hostility.
60%
4
Incitement High
Calls for discrimination, boycotts, exclusion, or harassment targeting Jewish individuals or communities.
80%
5
Threat / Violence Critical
Direct threats of violence, glorification of atrocities, or coordinated campaigns posing imminent danger.
100%
